ABOUT US

The amis of APU Basketball Club are provide an environment for students to unwind ensuring both physical and mental development. It also promotes positivity and respect for each other, not only creating better players, but better people. We also provide a safe and supportive environment to those students wish to develop their skills in the sport of basketball. We provide students with technical training in addition to teaching the values of teamwork, responsibility and self-discipline.
